Treatment Philosophy

Getting to the root.
Not just managing the branches.

01 — Root Cause

Root cause over symptom suppression

Symptoms are signals, not the problem. Depression, anxiety, burnout — these are the mind communicating that something is out of alignment. We ask why, not just what.

02 — Developmental

Your present is shaped by your past

We take a developmental lens to your history. Understanding the emotional and behavioral patterns that formed early and are still running in the background. Your present is shaped by your past. We look at both.

03 — Minimal Dose

Medication is a tool, not a treatment plan

Medication, when it is the right tool, is prescribed at the minimum effective dose that allows deeper work to happen. It is never the destination. For many patients, it is not needed at all.
